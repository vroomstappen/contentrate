Welcome to ContentRate 4

A fantasy investing game built around real content creator scores. Build a roster, run daily matches, and grow your CP (ContentPoints) balance.

📊

The Leaderboard

Browse all creators ranked by their 14-day average score. Click a card to see their full profile and match history. Recruit up to 4 at once.

⭐

Stars & the Crown

A gold star means highest score of the day. The crown marks the current King — whoever has the best form over their last 5 scores.

🔢

Form Points

Calculated from a creator's last 5 scores by colour band:

Purple ≥9.0+4
Blue 8.0–8.99+2
Green 7.0–7.99+1
Yellow 6.5–6.990
Orange 6.0–6.49−1
Red <6.0−2
💰

How CP is Calculated

When you hit Run Daily Match, each farmer earns or costs CP based on their latest score:

Score ≥ 9.0 — Jackpot+40 CP
Score ≥ 8.0 — Profit+20 CP
Score 7.0–7.99 — Partialup to +15 CP
Score 6.5–6.99 — Break even0 CP
Score < 6.5 — Penalty−15 CP

Captain (C) earns double rewards but also double penalties!

🎖️

The Captain

Assign one farmer as your Captain (C) from their profile. Their rewards and penalties are both doubled. In the global leaderboard, captains appear with a red (C) next to their name. Choose wisely!

🤝

Recruit Your Roster

Spend CP to recruit up to 4 creators. Releasing a farmer only returns 50% of their value — so choose carefully. Higher-rated creators cost more but can earn more too.

📉

Current Content Quality

At the bottom of the screen, a live gauge shows the Current Content Quality — the average of all creators' most recent scores. It also shows who had the best and worst score of the day.

🥇

Leaderboard & Compare

Create an account to appear on the global leaderboard and add a profile picture. Use Compare for a deep head-to-head breakdown including score momentum, leaderboard positions, valuation history and attribute DNA.
